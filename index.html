Background of CEST Nepal

CEST Nepal is a non Governmental and non-profit making organization committed to contribute to the sustainable tourism development and environmental management in Nepal. By getting registration at the District Administration Office Kathmandu, affiliated at the Social Welfare Council (SWC) Nepal and by getting the Permanent Account Number (PAN) from the Department of Internal Revenue, CEST Nepal has become a legally existed entity working in tourism and environment in Nepal. Established in August 2007 by some young and energetic professionals; CEST Nepal has build a positive image in Nepal for its contribution to the tourism development in Nepal.

Facilitating Tourism and Hospitality Management Training for tourism entrepreneurs and chamber member at Palpa for Nepal Tourism Board

On behalf of Nepal Tourism Board, CEST Nepal facilitated a two days training on Tourism and Hospitality Management Training for the tourism entrepreneurs and planners at Tansen, Palpa. The training programme was jointly organized by the Nepal Tourism Board and Palpa Chamber of Commerce and Industry (PCCI), Palpa in the eve of Trade fair and Tourism festival 2008. Mr. Ram Chandra Sedai, delivered sessions on tourism management and Mr. Paras Sharma delivered sessions on hospitality management.
